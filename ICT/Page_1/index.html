How dangerous is it to browse the dark web for normal users

• Using Tor without precautions or a secure system like Tails to browse illegal sites is very risky. Your real IP address could be exposed, and accessing illegal content can lead to legal trouble. Additionally, many dark web sites spread malware, and without proper security, your activities could be traced back to you.

How dangerous is it to browse in the dark web

Browsing the dark web can be dangerous for several reasons. It exposes you to illegal content, scams, and potential cyberattacks. Some sites may try to steal personal information or spread malware. Additionally, engaging in illegal activities can lead to legal consequences. Always proceed with caution and avoid risky behavior.
